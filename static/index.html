Food Bot NY

This app provides restaurants suggestions near Midtown in NY City based on cuisine input. The restaurants are sorted in increasing order of cost. The app uses Zomato Api's to get this information.

For support contact madhavnkhosla@gmail.com


Usage: /mycuisine CuisineName

Eg: /mycuisine Middle Eastern

/mycuisine Italian
